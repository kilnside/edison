## What Edison is

Edison is a set of markdown instructions (a "skill") that your AI coding assistant interprets. It guides the assistant through a structured design exploration process. Edison does not execute code, access external APIs, or modify your system beyond creating markdown files in your project directory.

## What Edison is not

Edison is not a substitute for professional design review, architecture consultation, security audit, or legal advice. The research Edison conducts uses your AI assistant's web search capabilities and is limited by the quality and recency of available sources. Research findings should be verified independently before making business or technical decisions.

## AI-generated output

All output from Edison (specs, research briefs, analyses, recommendations) is generated by your AI coding assistant following Edison's instructions. This output may contain errors, outdated information, or incorrect conclusions. You are responsible for reviewing and validating all Edison output before acting on it.
